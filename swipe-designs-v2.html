3 ways to show "this person has active posts" on the profile card

The card is profile-first (name, photo, bio, grades, gear). If the person has posted partner requests, we surface that — here are 3 approaches.
All use the Classic Stack (Option 1) as the base design.
A — Floating Post Banner A card at the top of the card. Tappable, shows post count + nearest date. Slides in when posts exist, hidden when none. Clear CTA to drill into posts.
